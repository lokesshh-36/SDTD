Objective of SDTD

  • To bring out data democratisation.
  • To develop digital lean business.
  • To develop and promote digital culture within the company.
  • To ensure optimal upgradation of technology.

Core Activities Conducted

  • Preparation required for hiring consultants and creating the SAIL Digitalization roadmap and implementation plan.
  • Organizing committee meetings for the Digitalization journey in SAIL.
  • Transferring successfully implemented use cases from one Plant/Unit to another.
  • Monitoring ongoing Digital initiatives across all Plants/Units.
  • Exploring and obtaining leading initiatives in the field of Digitalization for implementation in SAIL.
